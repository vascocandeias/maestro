Multivariate time series analysis brought online.

What can I do in this website?

The purpose of this tool is to analise multivariate time series using Dynamic Bayesian Networks online. It consists in the aggregation of multiple tools that were so far only available as offline command-line programs, which (let's be honest) you would otherwise probably not try.

Perfect! Now, how do I use it?

Tell us who you are by registering an email so that we know how to contact you.

Before analysing anything we need the data set, so upload a multivariate time series CSV file to get started.

You can now use as many tools as you want to analyze your data. Enjoy yourself!

Sometimes you need to give the tools time to do their job. But not to worry: if it takes too long, we will send you an email.

We have no use for your results. So when you are done, just download them and be on your way!

But why do I need an account?

Sometimes things take longer than we would like. In this tool, that would be the data analysis. And when that happens, we do not want you to keep a tab open, waiting to get the result. Imagine the caos when analyzing many data sets at the same time and all of them taking longer than one hour! This way, you can go on with your life and be notified when you get your results. Moreover, you will be able to come back later and access this results, until either you remove them or they are automatically deleted.

That's not reason enough for you? Would privacy be one?

We could still give you the result's link in advance and let you come back later and verify if it was ready, sure. But that would mean that anyone with that link could also check your result, as there would be no possible authentication. Anyone could guess the URL and just use your analysis. the same would happen with the data sets! By signing up, you can forget about those concerns and you will have control over everything you own. Plus, when you are done, deleting the account is even simpler than signing up!

Ok... So is my data safe?

Yes! The only thing we ask from you is a verified email, so that we can not only guarantee your legitimacy but also let you know whenever you have results waiting for you.

As for the time series data and results, it is safely stored in Amazon S3 and DynamoDB with AES-256 encryption at rest, secured from prying eyes. And everything you have in the servers is transparently shown in your dashboard, so that you can delete anything, even your entire account, at any time. If you forget to do so, they will still be automatically wiped after three days.

What if I just want to see what you are capable of?

No worries: we have some sample example data sets that can be used rapidly. You still need an account, but you can delete it as soon as you are finished. No strings attached.

And how do I format my own files?

It's easy. The main multivariate time series CSV files should have a header line with all of the attributes and time points separated by two underscores. These should be grouped by index and the order must be maintained. The remaining lines start with an identifier of each subject, which is followed by its observations. Missing values can be either empty, "?" or "NaN".

The following file is a very simple example of this format, and represents four subjects with two attributes (X and Y) evaluated across three time points. 

id,X__0,Y__0,X__1,Y__1,X__2,Y__2

10,High,Short,Low,Long,Low,Short
20,Low,Long,Low,Short,High,Short
30,Low,Short,High,Long,High,Long
40,Hgih,Long,High,Short,Low,Long

For information on other files specific to each package, view their respective instructional webpage.
